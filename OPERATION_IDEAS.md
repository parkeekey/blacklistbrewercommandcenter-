## Core Concept

The app is Mother Base (MGSV). The Operation Page is the Command Center.

Three phases:

### 1. Pre-Deployment (Intel / Briefing)
Before the run starts, assess:

- **Weather forecast** — already have weather logging, but pre-run intel?
- **Customer incentive** — high season / low season / events nearby
- **Flight scraper (future)** — scrape flight arrival data to predict nationalities walking by
- **☠️ Difficulty hunch** — manual 0–99 rating based on:
  - Extreme heat / rain / both
  - Customer emotion/incentive
  - Humidity + no nice weather
  - Comfortable → uncomfortable scale
  - Risk of "A-Agent" (coffee snob who bullies the stand)
- **Resist Level** — how prepared you feel:
  - Gear state (bluetooth scale, extraction monitoring, advanced brew setup)
  - Personal skill (SCA brewing, sensory taste)
  - Mom / neighbour shop around for backup
  - Events that inspire you
  - Has gone from 20-30 → 40 over 117 weeks of street operation

### 2. Deployment (The Run)
Already have: POS, sales, weather logging, downtime.

Potential additions:
- Live difficulty adjustment during run
- A-Agent encounter logging (snob sighting / bullying event)

### 3. After Action Report
End-of-run evaluation card:
- Forecast difficulty vs actual difficulty
- Did resist level help?
- Outcome rating (maybe not letter grades — could feel bad on bad days)
- Snapshot for Instagram story (no one else needs to understand)
